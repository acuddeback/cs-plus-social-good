ABOUT

The mission of EmblemEDU is to provide students with lifelong ownership of their official academic records, allowing students to store and share these records with any institutions and employers they choose. Our product will alleviate the tedious nature of the college admissions process on both guidance counselors and students, as well as expedite admissions decisions for colleges and universities. EmblemEDU ensures that the records remain secure, verified, and tamper-proof through cryptography and blockchain technology.

At EmblemEDU, we believe that every person has the right to own and personally share their academic achievements with institutions and employers to propel their lives forward.

There are three user types:

Guidance Counselors

Authenticated as trusted third parties using face recognition, guidance counselors need only to verify students’ transcripts via the blockchain.

Students

Every student has immediate access to their transcript and can share it with as many colleges as they like.

Admissions Officers

Admissions officers can easily view applicants’ transcripts and trust that it has been verified.
